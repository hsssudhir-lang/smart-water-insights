

# Smart Water Workshop Website — Implementation Plan

## Overview
A single-page, scroll-based landing website for the "Smart Water: From Technologies to Data Science" workshop. Modern, minimal design with a **Deep Teal / Navy / Aqua** color palette and a "Water × Data" scientific aesthetic. Sticky top navigation with smooth anchor scrolling.

---

## Navigation (Sticky Top Bar)
- Anchor links: Overview · Program · Speakers · Apply · Logistics · FAQ · Contact
- Primary CTA button always visible: **"Apply Now"** (links to Google Form)

---

## Sections (in scroll order)

### 1. Hero
- Workshop title, subtitle, date (06–08 April 2026), venue (ICSR, IIT Madras)
- Organizer names + space for logos (SPARC, IIT Madras, Tel Aviv University, KMCH)
- Two CTA buttons: **Apply / Submit EOI** (Google Form link) + **View Program** (anchor scroll)
- "Seats limited · Selection-based" badge

### 2. Quick Facts Bar
- Horizontal tiles: Dates · Venue · Mode (On-site) · Deadline · Fee range

### 3. About the Workshop
- Two paragraphs describing the workshop and its connection to People's Water Data

### 4. Why Attend (Key Takeaways)
- 6 benefit cards/bullets covering practical skills, hands-on exposure, reproducibility mindset
- Optional "deliverables" note about reproducible notebooks and documentation

### 5. Learning Themes (Icon Grid)
- 7 tiles with icons: Water treatment · Sensors & IoT · Data preprocessing · Time-series modeling · Geospatial analysis · GenAI · Multimodal fusion

### 6. Workshop Format
- Morning talks + afternoon hands-on explanation
- Scaffolded learning philosophy callout
- Note that detailed syllabus shared with shortlisted participants

### 7. Program Schedule (3-Day Tabs)
- Tab component: Day 1 / Day 2 / Day 3
- Each day shows time blocks with session type badges (Talk / Demo / Tutorial / Break)
- "Schedule subject to minor changes" disclaimer

### 8. Speakers & Resource Persons
- Card grid with: name, affiliation, topic/role
- Space to upload speaker photos later
- Separate subsection for hands-on instructors

### 9. Who Should Apply
- Target audience bullets (students, professionals, data scientists, policymakers)
- "You'll benefit most if…" section

### 10. Important Dates (Timeline)
- Visual timeline: Applications open → Close → Shortlist → Workshop dates

### 11. Fees & What's Included
- Fee table (Academic / PWD participants / Industry)
- Included items: sessions, materials, accommodation, food, certificate
- Travel note

### 12. How to Apply (Selection Process)
- 5-step visual pipeline: Submit EOI → Review → Shortlist → Pay → Attend
- "What we look for" criteria bullets
- Prominent **Apply CTA** button linking to Google Form

### 13. Venue & Logistics
- ICSR, IIT Madras description
- Sub-sections: How to reach · Campus entry · Accommodation details

### 14. Prerequisites & What to Bring
- Laptop, basic quantitative skills, optional Python setup
- Recommended background (not mandatory)

### 15. FAQ (Accordion)
- 6 common questions (online vs in-person, selection, certificate, fees, schedule changes, prerequisites)

### 16. Footer / Contact
- Email: peopleswaterdata@gmail.com
- Suggested subject line for queries
- Space for partner logos

---

## Design Direction
- **Colors**: Deep teal primary, navy/charcoal secondary, aqua/mint accents, off-white background
- **Typography**: Clean sans-serif (Inter), large headings, generous whitespace
- **Motifs**: Subtle water ripple / data grid patterns in hero background
- **Style**: Minimal, scientific, professional — optimistic and welcoming tone
- **Responsive**: Fully mobile-friendly single-page layout
